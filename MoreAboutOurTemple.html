HH Bhakti Rasamrita Swami

Temple President

His Holiness Bhakti Rasamrita Swami has been a dedicated disciple of His Holiness Jayapataka Swami since 1995. He took sannyasa vows in 2008 and has since been instrumental in establishing several ISKCON centers across Odisha.

Under his guidance, ISKCON Burla has grown from a small preaching center to a thriving temple with daily programs serving hundreds of devotees. He emphasizes the importance of book distribution and congregational development.

Before joining ISKCON, he completed his engineering degree from NIT Rourkela and worked briefly as a software engineer before dedicating his life fully to Krishna consciousness.

Gopal Das

Head Pujari

Gopal Das received his pujari training at ISKCON Mayapur and has been serving the deities at ISKCON Burla since 2010. His attention to detail and loving care for the deities is evident in their beautiful appearance and the temple's pristine altar.

He oversees a team of 8 assistant pujaris and conducts regular training programs for new devotees interested in learning deity worship. Under his guidance, the temple has maintained excellent standards of deity worship according to ISKCON guidelines.

Before joining ISKCON, Gopal Das studied fine arts at Sambalpur University, which helps him in decorating the deities with creative outfits and jewelry.

Radha Devi Dasi

Head Cook

Radha Devi Dasi joined ISKCON in 2005 and has been heading the temple kitchen since 2008. She specializes in preparing traditional Odia prasadam as well as North Indian and South Indian dishes for the deities.

She leads a team of 12 cooks who prepare daily offerings for the deities and Sunday feast for 300+ devotees. Her cooking classes for temple devotees are always fully booked months in advance.

Before dedicating her life to temple service, she ran a successful catering business in Sambalpur. She often says that cooking for Krishna is much more satisfying than cooking for any worldly client.

Nitai Chandra Das

Youth Coordinator

Nitai Chandra Das joined ISKCON as a youth volunteer in 2012 and became full-time in 2015 after completing his B.Tech degree. He coordinates all youth activities including weekly study groups, kirtan programs, and volunteer initiatives.

Under his leadership, the ISKCON Burla youth group has grown to over 200 active members who participate in various preaching and service activities. He also organizes the annual Youth Retreat that attracts participants from across Odisha.

His dynamic personality and ability to connect with young people have made him an effective ambassador for Krishna consciousness among the youth.

Our Temple Mission

The guiding principles that inspire our service to Srila Prabhupada and Lord Krishna

Scriptural Study

To systematically propagate spiritual knowledge to society at large and to educate all people in the techniques of spiritual life.

Community Service

To bring members closer together for the purpose of teaching a simpler, more natural way of life and to propagate Krishna consciousness.

Spiritual Development

To help people develop love for God through the chanting of the holy names and following the process of bhakti-yoga.
