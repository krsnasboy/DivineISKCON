The Blessings of Sponsoring Prasadam

In the Vedic tradition, feeding others is considered one of the highest forms of charity. When that food is first offered to Lord Krishna with love and devotion, it becomes prasadam—the Lord's mercy in the form of sanctified food.

By sponsoring the Sunday Feast, you participate in this sacred act of devotion. Your contribution ensures that hundreds of devotees and visitors can partake in this spiritual banquet, receiving both physical nourishment and spiritual upliftment.

Srila Prabhupada, the founder of ISKCON, emphasized that distributing prasadam is as important as distributing spiritual knowledge. Both satisfy the soul—one externally and one internally.

What Your Sponsorship Provides

Each Sunday Feast typically serves 200-300 devotees with a full vegetarian meal prepared according to Vedic standards. The menu usually includes:

  • Freshly prepared rice dishes
  • Flavorful vegetable preparations
  • Dal (lentil soup) and chapatis
  • Sweet rice or other desserts
  • Fruits and milk sweets

Your sponsorship covers all ingredients, preparation costs, and service materials needed to make this feast possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about sponsoring the Sunday Feast

What is the ideal time to book a sponsorship?

We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance to ensure proper planning. However, last-minute sponsorships may sometimes be accommodated depending on availability.

Can I sponsor in memory or honor of someone?

Absolutely. Many devotees sponsor feasts to honor birthdays, anniversaries, or in memory of departed loved ones. We'll happily announce your dedication during the feast.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept bank transfers, UPI payments, cheques, and cash. Online payment links can be provided upon request. All donations are eligible for 80G tax exemption.

Can I help prepare the feast I sponsor?

Yes! Many sponsors find great joy in participating in the cooking and serving. Please coordinate with our kitchen in-charge to arrange your participation.

What if I need to cancel my sponsorship?

We understand circumstances change. If you cancel at least 72 hours before your scheduled date, we can either refund your donation or apply it to a future date.
