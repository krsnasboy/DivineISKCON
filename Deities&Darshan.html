Deities & Darshan

Experience the divine presence of our beloved deities at ISKCON Burla

Our Divine Deities

The temple is blessed with beautiful deities who mercifully accept our devotion and prayers

Sri Sri Radha Krishna

The divine couple Radha and Krishna represent the perfect loving relationship between the soul and the Supreme. Their beautiful forms bless all who come for darshan.

Morning: 7:30 AM | Evening: 6:30 PM

Jagannath Baladev Subhadra

Lord Jagannath, the Lord of the Universe, along with His brother Baladev and sister Subhadra, bless devotees with their merciful wooden forms.

Morning: 8:00 AM | Evening: 7:00 PM

Gaura Nitai

Lord Chaitanya (Gaura) and Nityananda (Nitai) appeared 500 years ago to spread the chanting of the holy names throughout the world.

Morning: 7:45 AM | Evening: 6:45 PM

Daily Darshan Schedule

Regular timings to visit the temple and have darshan of the deities

Mangala Arati

4:30 AM - 5:00 AM

The first arati of the day, performed during the auspicious brahma-muhurta time.

Darshan Arati

7:30 AM - 8:30 AM

Morning darshan with arati ceremony and kirtan.

Raj Bhoga Arati

12:30 PM - 1:00 PM

Midday arati when the deities are offered their full meal.

Sandhya Arati

6:30 PM - 7:30 PM

Evening arati with beautiful kirtan and lamp offering.

Shayana Arati

8:30 PM - 9:00 PM

The final arati of the day when the deities retire.
