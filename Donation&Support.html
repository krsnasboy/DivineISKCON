Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about donating to ISKCON Burla

How will my donation be used?

All donations are used strictly for temple operations including deity worship, facility maintenance, prasadam distribution, educational programs, and community services. We maintain complete transparency in our financial reporting.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, ISKCON Burla is a registered charitable trust under Section 12A of the Income Tax Act. All donations are eligible for tax exemption under Section 80G. You will receive a receipt for your records.

Can I donate in memory of someone?

Absolutely. We offer memorial donations where you can honor a loved one. The temple will perform special prayers for their spiritual benefit.

How can I become a monthly donor?

You can set up recurring donations through our website or by contacting our donation coordinator. Monthly donors receive special updates and invitations to exclusive events.

What are the benefits of donating?

Beyond tax benefits, donors receive spiritual merits by supporting Krishna's mission. Major donors are recognized in our temple and receive special blessings from the deities.

Ready to Make a Difference?

Your support helps preserve Vedic culture and spread the message of Krishna consciousness in our community.
