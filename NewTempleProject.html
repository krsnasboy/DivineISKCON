Upcoming Temple Project

A divine initiative to establish a magnificent temple dedicated to Lord Krishna in Burla, Odisha

The Vision

Under the divine guidance of Srila Prabhupada, ISKCON Burla is embarking on an ambitious project to build a grand temple that will serve as a spiritual oasis for devotees and seekers alike. This temple will be a beacon of Krishna consciousness in the region, offering a sacred space for worship, education, and community service.

The proposed temple will feature traditional Vedic architecture combined with modern amenities, creating a harmonious blend of spirituality and functionality. Our vision is to create not just a place of worship, but a comprehensive spiritual center that serves the community through various outreach programs.

Main Temple

A majestic structure housing the deities of Sri Sri Radha Krishna, with a spacious altar and viewing area for devotees.

Vedic Library

A comprehensive collection of Vedic scriptures, Prabhupada's books, and multimedia resources for spiritual study.

Prasadam Hall

A large dining facility capable of serving hundreds of devotees with sanctified vegetarian meals daily.

Community Center

Multipurpose halls for spiritual gatherings, seminars, and cultural programs to engage the local community.

Project Highlights

The ISKCON Burla temple project will be a landmark spiritual destination featuring:

  • Traditional Vedic architecture with modern engineering
  • Eco-friendly design incorporating sustainable practices
  • Spacious prayer halls with capacity for 500+ devotees
  • Beautifully landscaped gardens and meditation areas
  • State-of-the-art sound system for kirtans and lectures
  • Accessible facilities for all devotees

Project Timeline

Our temple construction will proceed in carefully planned phases to ensure quality and sustainability:

January 2025

Land Acquisition Completed

Finalized the purchase of 2 acres of land in the heart of Burla, strategically located for easy access.

March 2025

Architectural Design Finalized

Completed the architectural plans with input from senior devotees and temple architects.

June 2025

Groundbreaking Ceremony

Official commencement of construction with Vedic rituals and blessings from senior devotees.

December 2025

Main Structure Completion

Completion of the main temple structure and beginning of interior work.

May 2026

Deity Installation

Grand installation ceremony for Sri Sri Radha Krishna deities with participation from devotees worldwide.

August 2026

Grand Opening

Inauguration of the temple with week-long celebrations, kirtans, and spiritual discourses.
