Upcoming Temple Project

A divine initiative to establish a magnificent temple for Lord Krishna in Burla

The Vision of ISKCON Burla Temple

Our dream is to create a spiritual oasis that will serve as a beacon of divine love and wisdom for generations to come

We are humbled and excited to announce the upcoming ISKCON Burla Temple project — a divine initiative to create a spiritual oasis dedicated to Lord Krishna and Srila Prabhupada's vision. This temple will be more than just a place of worship; it will be a center for spiritual education, cultural preservation, and community service.

The proposed temple will feature traditional Vedic architecture with modern amenities, housing beautiful deities of Radha Krishna, Jagannath Baladev Subhadra, and Gaura Nitai. The complex will include a goshala (cow protection center), a prasadam hall serving sanctified vegetarian meals, and a Vedic cultural center for education and events.

Located on a serene 2-acre plot in the heart of Burla, the temple will provide a peaceful retreat from the hustle of daily life, offering everyone the opportunity to connect with the divine through devotional service, kirtan, and spiritual discourses.

Temple Features

The temple complex will include several facilities to serve the spiritual needs of the community

Main Temple Hall

A beautifully adorned sanctum with marble deities of Radha Krishna, Jagannath Baladev Subhadra, and Gaura Nitai, where devotees can offer prayers and participate in arati ceremonies.

Vedic Library & Study Center

A comprehensive collection of Vedic scriptures and books by Srila Prabhupada, along with study rooms for group discussions and personal reflection.

Prasadam Hall

A spacious dining area serving delicious sanctified vegetarian meals to hundreds of visitors daily, prepared with love and devotion.

Goshala (Cow Protection)

A dedicated area for protecting and serving cows according to Vedic principles, with facilities for milk production and organic farming.

Children's Activity Center

A vibrant space for youth programs including Sunday School, arts and crafts, and cultural activities to nurture spiritual values from an early age.

Guest Accommodation

Comfortable rooms for visiting devotees and pilgrims, ensuring everyone can participate in temple programs without accommodation concerns.

Project Timeline

Our roadmap to completing this divine project

January 2024

Land Acquisition

Secured the 2-acre plot in Burla through generous donations from devotees.

March 2024

Architectural Design

Finalized the temple design with traditional Vedic elements and modern functionality.

June 2024

Groundbreaking Ceremony

Performed Vedic rituals to sanctify the land and begin construction.

September 2024

Foundation Completed

Finished the temple foundation with sponsored bricks from devotees worldwide.

December 2024

Superstructure Progress

Completed 50% of the main temple structure with beautiful carvings and domes.

March 2025 (Projected)

Deity Installation

Planned installation of Radha Krishna deities with grand consecration ceremony.

June 2025 (Projected)

Grand Opening

Inauguration of ISKCON Burla Temple with festivals and spiritual programs.
